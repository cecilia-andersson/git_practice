What Ceci Sees






Welcome!

Hi! I'm Ceci, and I see things. If you're here, you're probably me, because this website isn't live yet.

Growing up in southern California, the word hike paired in my mind with hot, exhausting, and dull. It took a few years of introducing myself to New England forests, rivers, and lakes before I fell in love with spending time outdoors. Now, most of my free time is spent fantasizing about the next mountain to climb, lake to bathe in, river to paddle. And expensive gear to buy. But, as a lover of purchases that are green (and account balances in the black), I'm working on that.



Browse my posts below and tag along with me as I discover more people, places, and things!

Hiking

Want to get into hiking but don't know where to start? Experienced hiker looking for hidden gems ? You're both in the right place! Here you'll find reviews of trails from wheelchair-accessible to I-can't-believe-we-did-that. Plus, tips on getting started, staying safe, and stories of my own learning curve.

Travel

Since even the most robust itineraries can't predict missed connections, stolen wallets, and hotel booking mistakes, it's good to know how to adapt your plans without losing your peace. I'll even share some of my proudest plans (and mistakes)!

Trail Recipes

It is a tragedy that food tastes the best after a long hike, but the lightweight meals you can carry backpacking are usually... lacking. As I'm spending more time far from fridges and multi-burner stoves, I'm perfecting some yummy recipes with a variety of ingredient weights and cooking tool requirements.
